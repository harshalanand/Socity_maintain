## 📊 **WHAT YOU NOW HAVE**

### ✅ **Society Members Management**
- Add, edit, delete members
- Track member status (Active, Inactive, Moved Out, Suspended)
- Search by name, email, phone
- Member statistics dashboard

### ✅ **Flat/Unit Management**
- Register all flats/units
- Support for 1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, 4BHK, Shops, Offices, Parking
- Building organization
- Floor and parking slot tracking
- Building overview reports

### ✅ **Maintenance Charges System**
- Configure charges per flat type
- Automatic monthly charge generation
- Track payment status (Pending, Paid, Partial, Overdue)
- Record payments with 4 methods (Cash, Cheque, Online, UPI)
- Member and flat-level summaries

### ✅ **Notices & Announcements**
- Post announcements (Maintenance, Emergency, General, Meeting)
- Set expiry dates
- Attach PDF documents
- Track visibility

### ✅ **Complaint Management**
- Members can file complaints
- Categories: Maintenance, Noise, Parking, etc.
- Priority levels: Low, Medium, High, Critical
- Assignment to staff
- Resolution tracking
- Statistics and analytics

### ✅ **Utilities Tracking**
- Water consumption
- Electricity tracking
- Gas monitoring
- Payment status

### ✅ **Committee Management**
- Register committee members
- Track positions and terms
- Contact information

### ✅ **Meeting Minutes**
- Document all meetings
- Record agenda and decisions
- Track action items
